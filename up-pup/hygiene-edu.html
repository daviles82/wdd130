Matting and skin care

Parasites: FLEAS, TICKS, & MITES OH MY! If a pet is not on prevention it’s more likely that the pet will have parasites. These creatures don’t just cause itching, they can also spread diseases, cause anemia, and cause severe hair loss and skin sensitivity in your fur baby. Not to mention, these buggers are zoonotic, which means that they can feed on you too. YIKES!

Teeth and anal health

Tooth Decay/Gum Disease: Dogs and cats have teeth just like we do, and just like us they need to be cared for often. Teeth brushing and yearly cleanings are a must for your pet’s teeth. If their teeth and gums are not cared for it can lead to plaque, cavities, tooth loss, gum disease and bone loss. Bad teeth and gums can also affect the cardio vascular system.
