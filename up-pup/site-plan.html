Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to bring family members with no voice a chance to live comfortably and healthy, we are talking about men’s best friend. Each member of the family knows how to wash up and care for themselves, but dogs don’t have that capacity. Our services are to bring comfort and happiness to as many dogs across the Katy area as possible.

Audience

Our audience are folks who want what is best for their pets. Pet parents don’t always get the necessary grooming and hygiene education while at a pet store or rescue facility. That is why this website was created to give them a chance to maximize the health of their furry little loved ones.

Content

Home page

At On The Pup & Up, we want to ensure that your pet’s grooming needs are on the up & up. We take our time to ensure that each pet has a pawsitive spa day, by scheduling one on one bonding time between your pet and their groomer before and after their grooming sessions. We believe trust between pet and groomer to be essential. We also use top of the line products to ensure your fur-baby’s skin & coat feel great and stay healthy. We take pride in our AKC S.A.F.E certified and Pet CPR certified staff. Not only are we safe and skilled, we are passionate about the health of your pet’s skin and coat, and getting them to look and feel their absolute best. We want what’s best for fido. After all it’s our business. Still have questions? Great! We’ve got answers. Head on over to our FAQ page for more info on our grooming process.

Images for the Home page

Grooming

Here on the On The Pup & Up, we offer a different type of service. One that considers your pet’s custom needs. Most other grooming services are timed crunch that will not consider if your pet is having trouble with their groom. Grooming service for a dog could be terrifying at times, we gently introduce the groom to avoid anxiousness.

Did you know that grooming isn’t only about looking good, it’s a matter of health? At On The Pup & Up, we use the S.C.E.N.T (Skin, Coat, Ears & Eyes, Nails, and Teeth) method to check your dog in before the appointment. This gives the groomer and the pet parent a good starting point regarding the custom care your pet will be receiving.
