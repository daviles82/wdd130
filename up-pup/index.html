What makes us tick

At On The Pup & Up, we want to ensure that your pet’s grooming needs are on the up & up. We take our time to ensure that each pet has a pawsitive spa day, by scheduling one on one bonding time between your pet and their groomer before and after their grooming sessions. We believe trust between pet and groomer to be essential. We also use top of the line products to ensure your fur-baby’s skin & coat feel great and stay healthy. We take pride in our AKC S.A.F.E certified and Pet CPR certified staff. Not only are we safe and skilled, we are passionate about the health of your pet’s skin and coat, and getting them to look and feel their absolute best. We want what’s best for fido. After all it’s our business. Still have questions? Great! We’ve got answers. Head on over to our FAQ page for more info on our grooming process.
