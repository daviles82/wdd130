Grooming

Did you know that grooming isn’t only about looking good, it’s a matter of health? At On The Pup & Up, we use the S.C.E.N.T (Skin, Coat, Ears & Eyes, Nails, and Teeth) method to check your dog in before the appointment. This gives the groomer and the pet parent a good starting point regarding the custom care your pet will be receiving. We’ll go over everything we see before and during the appointment and keep you in the loop during the process. You might be asking, “what would a groomer see on a dog besides fur?” Great question! Tooth Decay/Gum Disease: Dogs and cats have teeth just like we do, and just like us they need to be cared for often. Teeth brushing and yearly cleanings are a must for your pet’s teeth. If their teeth and gums are not cared for it can lead to plaque, cavities, tooth loss, gum disease and bone loss. Bad teeth and gums can also affect the cardio vascular system.
