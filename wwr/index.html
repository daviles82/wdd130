A thrill and a half

Our purpose is to get you out and about and experience only our ancestors have experienced before. We are not talking about a 9-5 in a climate-controlled office, a place you can only survive with mosquito repellent. The white waters of America, or how we like to call it, rough waters. White water rafting has been around for years and not always safe, but with new technology comes improved safety and reliability with our inflatable boats. Yes, before it was real rafts with paddles or oars. Now with safety gear in this day in age it’s like nature’s rollercoaster, without the long lines and wait times of course. Experience what our ancestors experienced but without fear. Bring the family age ten and older, there is no better time to go out and explore what you never expected to explore before. Rough water rafting.
